About

Project inspiration

This project has been inspired via inadequate availability of accessible information to the people especially those in the rural areas. There is a saying: if you want to keep anything away from a black man, put it in black and white. I feel there are a good number of people who desire information, but lack access to these desired information, such as building wealth, investing, politics, leadership and science to mention a few genre, this project is designed to bridge that gap.

With this project, they get to browse for the available books they intend to gain knowlege from, borrow the resources and read on it within the confine of their comfort, however the limitation could be the cost of getting smart-phones or access to the internet.

Hence, this has begat the motivation for building a library application which can help future leaders or anyone with the zest to access information but has limited reources available to them in their locale.
